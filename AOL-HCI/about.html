OUR HISTORY

We were originally a small company in the computer accessories industry formerly known as GOSKIN. We expand our product to a bigger level. we had the idea because we wanted to make this company bigger than it is now.

Along with the rapid development of technology and increasing consumer interest in accessory products, especially computer users, we have ventured to sell these products. The high market share of computer users, especially among gamers, created a new name for our company, namely GOLITECH, which will make all equipment products for computer needs.
